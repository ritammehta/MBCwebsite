Midnight Book Club

Have you ever looked out of your window and seen the first snow of the year?
Have you ever witnessed a reunion between a dog and its long-gone owner?
Have you ever slept in and then realized you had nothing to do?

That was all thanks to us. Those warm fuzzies are the feelings we’re responsible for distributing amongst an ever-more agitated and divided world population, and we do it nowhere in such concentrated form as in our FREE monthly shows.

So if you like the feeling you feel when you see a child share with another, smaller, child, come on out. See us. Laugh your face off and feel the warm fuzzies.

What we do

A 'lil bit of everything!

Shows!

Every month, we put on a FREE longform improv show for the University of Michigan community! How exciting! Past themes have ranged from "An Evening with Jason Mraz" to "Scheduling Conflict."

Competitions and Festivals!

We travel! We attend the COLLEGE IMPROV TOURNAMENT and SLATE COMEDY FESTIVAL every year! And boy do we love it!
